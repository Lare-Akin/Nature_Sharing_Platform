Put your hero photo in this folder.

Recommended:
- Filename: hero.jpg (or update the src in index.html to match your filename)
- Orientation: portrait or square works best (the frame is taller than wide)
- Minimum size: 1000x1250px for sharp display on large screens
- Format: JPG or WebP, optimised for web (aim under 500KB)

Once added, also update the alt text in index.html to describe what's in the photo —
this matters for accessibility (screen reader users) and SEO.
