About this space

A sanctuary built from shared stillness

Why Laré & Nature exists

The world moves faster than it should. Notifications, deadlines, noise — they crowd the edges of every quiet moment. Laré & Nature exists as a place to breathe.

Here, nature photographs are paired with words of wisdom and soft ambient sound. Not to be consumed, but to be sat with. Each image is an invitation to slow down, if only for a minute.

Community guidelines

Laré & Nature is a space built on trust, respect, and a shared love of the natural world. Every photograph submitted is a gift to this community. To keep this space peaceful and welcoming for everyone, we ask that all contributors honour the following guidelines.

Submit only nature photography. This platform is dedicated to the natural world — landscapes, plants, wildlife, water, sky, seasons, and light. Photographs should centre on nature as their subject. Images of people, urban scenes, or unrelated content will not be approved.

No inappropriate or harmful content. We will not approve inappropriatecontent, graphic violence, or anything that could cause distress to visitors seeking calm.

Only share images you own or have rights to. Only submit photographs you took yourself, or images you have explicit permission to share. Do not submit work belonging to another photographer without their consent. Copyright matters and is taken seriously.

Be honest in your descriptions. The words of wisdom and image descriptions you provide should be genuine. Do not use the submission form to post advertisements, spam, links, or promotional content of any kind.

Respect the spirit of this space. Laré & Nature exists to help people slow down, breathe, and find stillness. Every submission should contribute to that feeling. If in doubt, ask yourself would this bring someone peace?

Curator decisions are final. All submissions are reviewed by a curator before appearing in the gallery. Right reserved to decline any submission that does not align with these guidelines, without obligation to provide a reason.

Thank you for being part of this community. Your contribution however small helps create something meaningful for people who need it most.

How it works

Anyone can submit a photograph of nature; a mist covered valley, a solitary tree, the last light on the water etc. Alongside the image, contributors share words: a reflection, a quote, a line that captures the feeling of being there.

A curator reviews every submission before it appears in the gallery. This keeps the space intentional. Not everything needs to be fast, or plentiful. Quality over quantity, always.

The soundscapes

While viewing a photograph, you can choose an ambient soundscape - forest birdsong, gentle rain, ocean waves, mountain wind, or the resonance of Tibetan bells. Or you can sit in silence. The choice is yours, as it should be.

Accessibility

Laré & Nature is built to be experienced by everyone. Every photograph has a written description for screen readers, controls can be used with a keyboard alone, and the site respects your system's motion preferences. If you encounter any barriers, please let me know.

Interactive relaxation arena

A space built for the moments when the world asks for too much. An interactive relaxation space where you control every element of your experience. Choose from background imagery, calming affirmations, a breathing spiral, and ambient soundscapes including gentle rain, ocean waves, forest sounds, meditation bells, or white noise. You can even load your own custom images and sounds. When everything is set to your liking, start a session, go fullscreen, and simply breathe..

Contact and feedback

For any feedback or request to remove your image, please contact us at akinlare@gmail.com.
